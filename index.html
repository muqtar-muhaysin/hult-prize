About Hult Prize at University For Development Studies-Tamale

The Hult Prize is a global competition that empowers students to solve pressing social issues through innovative and sustainable business ideas. At University For Development Studies-Tamale, we aim to nurture talent, foster innovation, and connect like-minded changemakers to transform communities.
